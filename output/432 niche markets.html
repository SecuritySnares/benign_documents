Global Niche Markets

Small specialised parts of the global market. On a country by country basis they do not offer enough market value to be successful. However the combination of all the niche markets in each country allow for the scale ability to present profits.

These types of markets are more likely to associated to G/S that use differentiation as their competitive advantage. They need to be specialised enough to meet individual needs and wants, but need the ability to up scale to make it profitable. This is why this often suits luxury markets. Whereby the product is what presents the competitive advantage, as the consumers will pay the premium price. The exclusivity often supports the niche market demands for being unique.
