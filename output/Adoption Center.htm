Adoption Center – Dog & Cat Adoptions

Dog adoptions and intake temporarily suspended

Pinellas County Animal Services has temporarily suspended dog adoptions and intakes to isolate and care for our shelter dogs following the detection of Canine Pneumovirus. We will continue to offer cat intake, spay/neuter and adoptions as the virus does not transmit among species. Learn more…

Adoptions make everyone smile at Pinellas County Animal Services. For a small fee, you can choose a dog or cat to be a part of your family. Adoptable animals are vaccinated and sterilized before moving to their new homes.

Vets Adopt Pets Program

We work along with VetsAdoptPets.org to celebrate and thank Veterans for their service to all Americans. As a show of our appreciation, we waive adoption fees for Veterans adopting pets from our shelter. Veterans must show proof of military status and meet our adoption requirements.
