Keep your loved ones safe by setting alert thresholds for certain drivers. If the vehicle is driven past a certain time, speed, or designated boundary, this system sends an alert to your smartphonee.

Parking Minder2

911 Connect4

In an emergency, we're here to help by offering peace of mind and assistance in certain situtations. If an airbag deploys, Kia eServices will attempt to automatically call 911 and send the vehicle's location to an emergency response agent4.

What is Kia eServices?

Kia eServices is Kia’s suite of connected vehicle services and features designed to provide you with more convenience, comfort, and connectivity.

What is Kia eServices?

Kia eServices is a subscription-free infotainment and telematics service available on select Kia vehicles. The system allows users to make hands-free calls on their smartphone, stream music, navigate to saved points of interest, and perform vehicle diagnostics with the use of voice commands. Kia eServices uses your smartphone to connect to your vehicle’s data via a USB cable.

What is the difference between Kia Connect and Kia eServices?

Kia eServices requires a compatible phone to be connected to the vehicle via a USB cable. Kia eServices provides features such as My POIs1, Vehicle Diagnostics2 Parking Minder3, and more, accessed through the Kia eServices app. Kia Connect is an embedded system with a modem built into the vehicle that supports all the functionalities of Kia eServices plus a variety of features like remote commands4, Find My Car5, and many more through the Kia Access app and Kia Owner’s Portal

What Kia eServices-equipped vehicles are appointed with Kia eServices?

Please check for Kia eServices availability on your vehicle by going to the Kia Connect Availability page on Kia Owner’s Portal.

How do I download the Kia eServices app?
  1. Go to iTunes®6 or Google Play.™7
  2. Search for and download the Kia eServices app.
  3. Install the app.
  4. Log in and register via the app (See “How do I register my Kia eServices account via the eServices app?” below).

 

How do I register my Kia eServices account via the eServices app?
How do I register my Kia eServices account via the eServices app? 
  1. Launch the Kia eServices app on your smartphone.
  2. Click on " Register."
  3. Enter your email, password, and mobile phone number.
  4. Read and agree to the Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy, and click “Next.”
  5. Return to the vehicle and follow the instructions for in-car Kia eServices setup.

 

I enrolled my car but my owner account does not show the car as Kia eServices enrolled?

Please check to see whether you have created two Owner's Portal accounts during the enrollment process. It may be the case where one account is associated with your mobile number and the other with your email ID. For further assistance, please contact the Kia Connect Call Center at (844) 886-9411.

Is Kia eServices available to pre-owned Kia customers?

Yes, Kia eServices-equipped pre-owned Kia customers will be able to enjoy eServices benefits. If you have issues setting up or using the system, please call our Kia Connect Call Center at (844) 886-9411 for more details.

I am selling my vehicle – what should I do if I am currently using eServices?

To deactivate your account, please call the Kia Connect Call Center at (844) 886-9411.
