7 Best SEO Tips for Niche Markets to Drive More Franchise Leads

01 July 2020

Attracting prospects to niche-specific markets is challenging but essential for franchise businesses. Many franchise businesses that are into niche markets are struggling to get leads due to a rather tiny audience.

The key to niche markets is Search Engine Optimization (SEO). Here are 7 SEO tips for niche markets that attract improved franchise leads.

1. Analyze the Search Volume of Your Niche Market

The search volume in your niche market will help you understand whether people are searching for products or services like yours or using more specific keywords.

2. Research Your Competition

Franchise businesses in niche markets need to research their competitors, since all of these businesses are after the same tiny market. Competitor analysis helps in understanding what they are doing better to attract their customers.

You need not copy your competitors, Instead, understand their SEO strategies and improvise it 10x.

3. Do Your Keyword Research

Keyword research is one of the all-important SEO tasks for niche markets. You need to understand and choose the relevant keywords and phrases that work for both your niche and location, and optimize your website content for these keywords.

This will help improve your Google ranking, resulting in increased franchise website traffic, franchise leads, and sales.

4. Create Your Franchise Niche-Specific Content

Unique website content is essential to improve your website SEO and search ranking. You may create niche-specific blogs, tutorials, user guides, and other useful and engaging content for your target audience.

5. Include the Keywords in Your Content

Once you have the current keywords list, you can create content that includes these keywords and publish on your website and other content-sharing platforms.

Doing this will increase your backlinks, website traffic, and search ranking. Remember, keywords can also be included in titles, meta descriptions, URL, image tags, graphics, etc.

6. Perform Technical SEO

Technical SEO helps your franchise business analyze the usability and functionality of your website. Pay attention to page speed, XML sitemap, mobile-friendliness, relevant links, etc.

7. Track Performance

It is important to track your franchisor website performance after incorporating these SEO tips. Use Google Analytics to track your overall website performance.
