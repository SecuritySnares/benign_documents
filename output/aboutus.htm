About Us

MindShare Technologies, Inc. is an established information technology consulting and staffing firm
based in Chicago, providing resource and project management support in a wide range of industries
and technological environments. Since it's founding in 2001 MindShare Technologies has succesfully delivered an array of innovative and cost-effective business and technical solutions for it's clients.
Whether you are looking to temporarily augment your project resource capacity or are looking to add additional permanent staff members, MindShare Technologies is here to help you achieve your goals.

Our focus is to provide highly skilled consultants at the most reasonable rates possible. Cost effective rates are the product of low corporate overhead as well as reasonable financial expectations on the
part of our consulting staff. When you are partnering with MindShare Technologies you are getting the most out of your very important Information Technology consulting budget.
